Frequently Asked Questions

Which technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in MERN stack, Java (Spring Boot), Python (Django/Flask), Kotlin app development, WordPress, and modern UI/UX design tools like Figma and Adobe XD.

Do you offer custom software development?

Yes! We build tailor-made software solutions for startups and enterprises based on their specific business needs, from web apps to automation tools.

Can you help redesign or improve my existing website?

Absolutely. Whether you're looking for UI/UX enhancements, responsive design upgrades, or backend refactoring — we’ve got you covered.

How does your digital marketing service work?

We use SEO, paid ads, review boosting, and social media marketing to improve your brand’s visibility and drive quality traffic and leads.
